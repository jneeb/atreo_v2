OUR STORY

ATREO ENTERTAINMENT

is a Canadian entertainment company created by Sunny Nakra. The name Atreo comes from the Greek word “King” while the three rubies logo was created to honour Nakra’s grandfather Ram Lal Dua, as Lal translates to “Ruby” in Hindi. Both the name and the logo speak to the nature of Atreo’s goals as a company, which is why we use experiences to create only the most memorable moments.
