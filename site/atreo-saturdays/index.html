GET ON THE GUESTLIST

Our guestlist is now EXCLUSIVE to bottle service reservations. To be put on the list, please submit a list of names (First and Last) according to booth size; 6, 10, 12 guests.

This will also include LINE BY PASS for all 6, 10 or 12 individuals booked in the booth. For special requests (Birthdays / Celebrations) people can contact atreoentertainment@gmail.com and allow up to 24 hours to respond.

When arriving at the venue, please go to the guestlist line and present your government issued ID. Management has the right to refuse entry.

DRESS CODE

Dress code is fashionable attire (Dress to impress). We do not permit hats, touques, flip-flops, sunglasses, fanny packs, athletic wear, shorts, track pants or baggy clothing. Security reserves all rights to refuse entry.
